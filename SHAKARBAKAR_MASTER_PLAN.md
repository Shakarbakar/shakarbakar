# SHAKARBAKAR MASTER PLAN

## Project Definition

ShakarBakar is a Football Ownership & Prediction Strategy Game.

Users receive virtual Bucks and use them to own football teams, make predictions, compete against other users, compete against Coaches, climb leaderboards, and follow football tournaments.

---

## Core Philosophy

The game must be simple enough for an 8-year-old child to understand.

If a feature cannot be explained to an 8-year-old in less than 30 seconds, it should be simplified.

---

## Target Audience

Primary:

* Football fans
* Ages 8+

Secondary:

* Casual football viewers
* Friends and family competitions

---

## Supported Languages

## Virtual Currency

Name:
Bucks

Starting Balance:
10,000 Bucks

No real-money deposits.

No real-money withdrawals.

---

## Team Ownership

Users can:

* Buy teams
* Sell teams
* Own multiple teams

The phrase "I own Germany" is part of the game identity.

---

## Team Pricing

Base Price:
500 Bucks

World Cup Bonus:
+100 Bucks per World Cup title.

Examples:

Brazil = 1000
Germany = 900
Argentina = 800
France = 700
