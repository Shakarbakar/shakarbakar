Everything you need to know to build your football empire.

👥 Friends & Chat

How do I add a friend?
Go to Arena → Arena Chat → Player Directory. Browse the players list or search by username. Press Add Friend beside the player you want to add. The player will receive a friend request. Once accepted, both players appear in each other's Friends List.
How can I send a private message?
First add the player as a friend. After the request is accepted, open Arena → Arena Chat → Private Chat. Select your friend and start chatting instantly.
How do I find other players?
Open Arena → Arena Chat → Player Directory. You can browse all registered players or search by username.
What happens when I receive a friend request?
Go to Arena → Arena Chat → Friend Requests. You can accept or decline incoming requests. Accepted players become friends and can communicate privately.
Can I chat like WhatsApp?
Yes. ShakarBakar includes a private messaging system that allows players to exchange messages instantly while discussing football, teams and duels.
What is the Arena Chat Hub?
Arena Chat Hub is the social center of ShakarBakar. It allows players to meet football fans, add friends, join conversations and participate in football communities.

⚔️ Prediction Duels

How do Prediction Duels work?
Go to Arena → Prediction Duels. Choose an opponent and send a duel invitation. Both players submit predictions and the winner is determined when the real result is known.
Can I see the Duel question before accepting?
Yes. The duel question is displayed before you submit your prediction so you know exactly what challenge you are accepting.
How is the winner determined?
After the actual result is submitted and confirmed, the system compares both predictions and determines the winner automatically.
What happens if both players predict correctly?
The duel ends as a draw.
What happens if both players predict incorrectly?
The duel also ends as a draw.
What is Arena Score?
Arena Score measures your performance in Prediction Duels. Winning duels improves your Arena Score and ranking.

🏆 Marketplace

How do I buy a national team?
Go to Marketplace. Browse available teams and open a team card to view its details. Press Buy Team to purchase the team using your Bucks balance.
Can I sell a team after buying it?
Not yet. The current version focuses on team ownership. Future marketplace updates may allow player-to-player transfers and team trading.
Can I own more than one team?
Yes. Players can build a portfolio of multiple national teams and expand their football empire.
What are Bucks?
Bucks are the in-game currency of ShakarBakar. They are used to purchase teams and may be used in future features and competitions.
What is the Starter Pack?
Every new player receives a Starter Pack when creating an account. It provides the initial Bucks needed to begin building a football empire.
What does Winning Chance mean?
Winning Chance is an indicator of a team's relative strength and probability of success. Higher values generally represent stronger football nations.
Why do teams have different prices?
Team prices are based on factors such as football history, achievements, World Cup titles, strength and popularity.

ℹ️ General

Is ShakarBakar free to play?
Yes. Creating an account, adding friends, chatting, participating in duels and exploring the platform are completely free.
How do I start as a new player?
Create an account, receive your Starter Pack, explore the Marketplace, enter the Arena and begin building your football network.
Can I play without buying teams?
Yes. You can use social features, chat with friends and participate in many platform activities without owning a team.
Can I change my username later?
Username management will be available through future Profile improvements.
I found a bug. What should I do?
Contact the platform administrator and provide:

• The page where the issue occurred
• The action you performed
• A screenshot if possible

This helps us improve ShakarBakar faster.
