About SHAKARBAKAR

SHAKARBAKAR is a football ownership and prediction game where fans can build their own football empire using virtual Bucks.

What is SHAKARBAKAR?

Buy teams.

Make predictions.

Challenge friends.

Compete in duels.

Climb the rankings.

And enjoy football in a whole new way.

No real money is required.

No real money can be won.

SHAKARBAKAR is designed purely for fun, strategy, and friendly competition.

How It Works

Every player starts with Bucks, the virtual currency of SHAKARBAKAR.

With your Bucks, you can:

  • Buy national teams
  • Sell teams
  • Build a portfolio
  • Make football predictions
  • Challenge other players
  • Compete in rankings

The goal is simple:

Grow your football empire and become one of the top managers in the community.

Team Ownership

In SHAKARBAKAR, football teams can be owned.

Want to own Brazil?

Go ahead.

Want to own Germany, France, and Argentina at the same time?

You can do that too.

Multiple users can own the same team, but each user can only own a team once.

Your collection becomes part of your identity across the platform.
